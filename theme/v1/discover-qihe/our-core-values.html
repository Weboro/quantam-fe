Our Core Values

At QIHE, our core values guide us in achieving our vision and mission:

  1. Academic Excellence: We are dedicated to delivering education that empowers students and prepares them for success in a global workforce.
  2. Equity and Inclusivity: We believe in providing equitable access to quality higher education, fostering an inclusive environment for all.
  3. Community Engagement: We strive to make a positive impact on our local community and beyond through meaningful contributions.
  4. Integrity: We uphold the highest standards of academic and operational integrity, ensuring transparency, compliance, and consistency.
  5. Accountability: We take responsibility for our actions and their impact, maintaining the trust of our students, stakeholders, and community.
  6. Sustainability: We are committed to financial and environmental sustainability, integrating responsible practices across our operations and curriculum.
  7. Adaptability: We embrace innovation and remain flexible to meet the challenges of a dynamic, ever-changing world.
  8. Global Outlook: We cultivate an international perspective in our programs and operations, fostering collaboration and global connections.
